Workflow

How TrustCheck Works

A seamless integration that works entirely in the background until you need it.

01. Extract
📄

Read the Page

Navigate to a product. Our content script instantly extracts titles, ratings, and up to 20 recent review bodies directly from the DOM.

02. Process
🧠

AI Analysis

The background worker passes data to our AI model, scoring sentiment, identifying fake patterns, and extracting specific buyer concerns.

03. Decide
⚖️

Instant Verdict

Within seconds, you receive a Trust Score and a definitive recommendation inside the Chrome popup. No more guessing.

Intelligence

What We Detect

TrustCheck evaluates 8 distinct heuristics to calculate the final Trust Score.

⭐

Star Rating

Used as a baseline modifier. Boosts high-rated products, penalizes low-rated ones before applying fake review adjustments.

Baseline Modifier
😊

Sentiment Ratio

Each review is scored against curated wordlists of positive and negative signals. The core component of the trust score.

50% Weight
🤖

Fake Phrase Density

Checks for templated fake-review phrases like "best product ever" or "highly recommend" common in paid review farms.

22+ Triggers
📏

Review Length

Very short reviews (under 8 words) are a red flag. A high ratio of short reviews significantly lowers the authenticity score.

Catches lazy fakes
🔁

Duplicate Detection

Reviews are compared using Jaccard Similarity. High word overlap (>65%) across multiple reviews flags as near-duplicates.

Bot Signal
💬

Vagueness Score

Positive, short reviews containing only generic praise with zero product-specific detail are flagged as vague.

Catches soft fakes
❗

Exclamation Density

Fake reviews frequently overuse exclamation marks to perform enthusiasm. The average count per review is penalized.

Over-enthusiasm
🎯

Concern Extraction

Scans via regex for 11 concern categories: durability, fake claims, size mismatch, delivery damage, battery issues, etc.

Actionable Insights
Outputs

Clear, Actionable Advice

✅

BUY

Trust Score ≥ 65

Reviews look genuine and specific. Positive sentiment dominates without suspicious patterns. Safe to purchase.

⚠️

CAUTION

Trust Score 39–64

Mixed signals detected. Some reviews appear genuine, others are vague or templated. Compare alternatives first.

🚫

AVOID

Trust Score ≤ 38

High fake review density, heavy complaints, or suspicious uniformity. Risk is too high. Look elsewhere.

Get Started

Install in 60 Seconds

Since we are in beta, TrustCheck is installed via Developer Mode. It's safe and takes 1 minute.

1

Download the Package

Click the download button and save the ZIP file to your computer.

Download extension.zip
2

Unzip to a Permanent Folder

Extract the contents. Crucial: Keep this folder somewhere safe, if you delete it later, Chrome will disable the extension.

trustcheck-extension/
3

Enable Developer Mode in Chrome

Open Chrome's extension page and toggle "Developer mode" on in the top right corner.

chrome://extensions/
4

Load Unpacked

Click the "Load unpacked" button at the top left and select the folder you unzipped in Step 2. You're done!
